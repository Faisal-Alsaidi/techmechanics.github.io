About Us
Tech Mech™ is a fresh startup located in Windsor, Ontario, that specializes in repairing and building computers for all types of users, guiding customers to get the perfect computer for their daily lives. Whether you need a gaming computer designed to play high-end VR titles or personal computers for writing documents in Microsoft Word, we'll build the PC that's right for you!
In addition to building and repairing PCs, we will do anything from securing your system to troubleshooting software problems to installing a new OS. If you don't know if we can do it, just ask! Our team of employees will gladly let you know what services we do and do not have and will guide you in understanding how to utilize our services.
Products
Our main pre-built computer is a gaming PC designed to play any of the most high-end games, capable of running anything from heavy VR games, intense shooters, and even virtual machines! Designed to be a long lasting gaming PC with all the newest specs, it will surely last you several years, playing any of the most heavy games in 5, 10, even 15 years!
With a computer case bolstering a huge glass panel, all the parts within your computer can easily be displayed from beneath your desk. Additionally, with an 850W power supply powering an Intel Z390 Express Chipset, your computer can run incredibly smoothly while also housing 3 USB Type-C ports, 3 USB Type-A ports, and 1 HDMI port. The Intel I9-9900K processor, with a max turbo speed of approximately 5.0GHz, 4 16GB DDR4 DRAM sticks of RAM, and the GeForce GTX 1660 Ti graphics card, the computer will be able to easily handle many incredibly high-end games with air cooling fans keeping it from overheating.
In addition to the computer itself, you'll be able to play your games on an MSI Optix MAG241C 24" curved monitor along with a wireless gaming mouse and mechanical keyboard. On top of all these accessories include a pair of HyperX Cloud Stinger Gaming Headphones, a 930mm wide mouse pad, 2 USB desktop speakers, as well as 1 year of ESET NOD32 Antivirus and 1 year of Microsoft Office 365. On top of all this, the computer will be pre-installed with Play on Linux, a Linux distro based on Wine designed to act as a high-end gaming OS. Without all the unnecessary software on a normal Windows machine, Play on Linux will allow you to turn your computer into a focused gaming PC, all while using any Windows applications as you normally would on a Windows machine. All for only $3,499.99!
Services
In addition to general computer repairs and building custom PCs, Tech Mech™ also provides a variety of different services which are accessible at any of our retail stores across Windsor!
If you come in with a laptop or desktop computer, we will provide a general security check to ensure your computer is secured against hackers and viruses. Each one of our employees are trained in cybersecurity to detect any vulnerabilities that could put your computer at harm, so we will gladly do a check to ensure you stay safe! Prices may vary depending on how many vulnerabilities are discovered.
Have you ever found that your computer is having trouble doing a basic task or won't function properly? Well, if you bring in your computer or laptop we will attempt a basic diagnostic assessment to see what the problem might be and attempt a fix. While there will be some problems we can't fix, we will do all that we can to find the problem and squash any bugs. If we can't fix your computer though, don't worry! Any unsolved problems will come free of charge, although prices may vary for issues that are found and fixed.
Want to try a new OS? Was your old OS broken and you need to set up a new one? If you bring a laptop or computer into one of our stores we will gladly install your OS onto your computer - whether it be Windows or Linux, we do it all! Mac computers are not supported for this service, and prices may vary depending on the OS.
